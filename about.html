Built on Trust & Expertise

Fahad Air Conditioning Comapny was founded in the heart of Riyadh's King Faisal District with a single mission: fast, honest, and professional cooling solutions for every home and business in the city.

What began as a small team of two certified engineers has grown into one of Riyadh's most trusted AC service providers — a full fleet of equipped vans, factory-certified technicians, and thousands of satisfied customers across all districts.
