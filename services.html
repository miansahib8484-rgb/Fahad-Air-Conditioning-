Common Questions

كم يستغرق وصول الفني؟How fast does a technician arrive?
▾
In most Riyadh areas our technicians arrive within 30–60 minutes of your call. For night emergencies, we aim for under 45 minutes. We dispatch the nearest available engineer immediately.
هل الفحص مجاني؟Is the diagnostic inspection free?
▾
Yes — on-site diagnosis is completely free when you book a repair. We explain the fault clearly and provide a transparent quote before any work begins. You approve everything first.
ما هي أنواع المكيفات التي تصلحونها؟What AC types do you service?
▾
We service all types: split units, window units, cassette, ceiling concealed, central / ducted, VRF multi-split, and portable units. All major brands — Gree, Samsung, LG, Carrier, Midea, Daikin, Hitachi, Toshiba and more.
هل تقدمون ضماناً على الإصلاحات؟Do repairs come with a warranty?
▾
Yes. All repairs come with a written service warranty. Major parts (compressors, PCBs, motors) carry manufacturer warranties where applicable. If the same fault recurs in the warranty period, we return at no charge.
