Common Questions

كم يستغرق وصول الفني إليّ؟How long until a technician arrives?
▾
In most Riyadh areas, our technicians arrive within 30–60 minutes of your call. For night emergencies we target under 45 minutes. We dispatch the nearest available certified engineer immediately.
هل الفحص والتشخيص مجاني؟Is the diagnostic inspection free?
▾
Yes — the on-site diagnosis is completely free when you book a repair. We explain the fault clearly and provide a transparent written quote before any work begins. You approve first, always.
هل تعملون أيام الجمعة والأعياد؟Do you work on Fridays and public holidays?
▾
Absolutely — we operate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week including Fridays, Ramadan, Eid Al-Fitr, Eid Al-Adha, and all Saudi public holidays. AC emergencies don't follow a schedule, and neither do we.
هل يمكنني إرسال صورة المشكلة عبر واتساب؟Can I send a photo of the issue via WhatsApp?
▾
Yes — sending a photo or short video via WhatsApp helps us pre-diagnose the issue and prepare the correct parts before arriving. This saves time and often means the repair is completed in a single visit.
ما المناطق التي تخدمونها في الرياض؟Which areas of Riyadh do you cover?
▾
We cover all Riyadh districts — Al-Malaz, Al-Rawdah, Al-Yasmin, Al-Olaya, Al-Narjis, Al-Shifa, Ad-Diriyah, Al-Wurud, Al-Hamra, Al-Murabba, City Centre, King Faisal District, and more. If you're in Riyadh, we reach you.
